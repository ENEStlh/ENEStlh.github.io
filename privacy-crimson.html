Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Overview

Crimson is a daily murder mystery game. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used. We are committed to being transparent and keeping your information safe.

By using Crimson, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Data We Collect

Crimson collects a minimal amount of data necessary to operate the game:

  • Usage data — how you interact with the app (screens visited, features used, session length), collected anonymously via Firebase Analytics
  • Crash reports — technical error information to help us fix bugs, collected via Firebase Crashlytics
  • Game progress — your puzzle completion status and scores, stored in Firebase Firestore to sync your progress
  • Advertising identifiers — used by Google AdMob to serve relevant ads (see Advertising section below)

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifying information unless you contact us directly.

How Puzzles Are Generated

Every daily mystery is generated on our servers using the OpenAI API — before it reaches your device. Your personal data, gameplay choices, and device information are never sent to OpenAI. The AI only receives our creative prompt; it never sees anything about you.

Advertising

Crimson uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect and use your advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS) to show you relevant advertisements.

You can limit ad tracking at any time in your device settings under Privacy & Security → Tracking. If you opt out, you will still see ads but they will not be personalized.

AdMob's data practices are governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Retention

Anonymous analytics data is retained for up to 14 months as per Firebase's default settings. Game progress data is retained as long as you use the app. You can request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

Crimson is rated 13+ on the App Store. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.
